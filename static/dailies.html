Dailies

  • Leg Circles

    Lie on your back with your arms by your sides, palm down. Point your left foot to the ceiling and rotate foot outward slightly. Trace a circle on the ceiling with your leg, making sure you keep your hips still and anchored to the floor but move your entire leg.

    • Complete: 5 sets of 30 reps
    Leg Experience: +50 exp


  • Lunge Walks

    Start standing with your feet together about hip-width apart and keep your back straight. Step forward into a lunge position with your thigh parallel to the floor, avoiding any tilting or swaying of the upper body and keeping your back braced and abdominal muscles engaged.

    • Complete: 5 sets of 30 reps
    Leg Experience: +50 exp


  • Pistol Squat

    Stand with feet hip-width apart with your elbows bent and hands formed into fists in front of your chest. Lift your right foot a few inches making sure it is flexed. Squat down, bending your left knee 90 degrees whilst lifting your other leg in front of you until it is level with your hips.

    • Complete: 5 sets of 30 reps
    Leg Experience: +50 exp


  • Calf Raises

    Start about 12 inches away from a wall with your toes facing forward and place your hands on the wall in line with your shoulders. Rise up slowly onto your toes, keeping your knees straight and avoiding rotating of the feet. Your hands should also be used for support. Hold this position for a couple of seconds then slowly lower your heels back to the floor.

    • Complete: 5 sets of 30 reps
    Leg Experience: +50 exp
